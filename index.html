Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to SIM Owner Info Tracker. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our application.

Information We Collect

When you use SIM Owner Info Tracker, we collect and process the following information:

  • Search history of phone numbers and CNIC numbers
  • Device information for app functionality
  • Usage statistics to improve our service

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Providing SIM owner information search functionality
  • Maintaining search history for user convenience
  • Improving our services and user experience
  • Ensuring app security and preventing misuse

Data Storage

Your search history is stored locally on your device using secure storage methods. We do not upload or store your personal search history on our servers.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Local storage encryption
  • Secure API communications
  • Regular security updates

User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Clear your search history at any time
  • Control what information is stored locally
  • Request information about your data usage

Updates to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on this page.
